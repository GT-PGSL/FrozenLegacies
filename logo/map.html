Frozen Legacies Interactive Map

This interactive map provides access to digitized radar flight lines and data from the 1974–75 SPRI/TUD/NSF airborne surveys over the Ross Ice Shelf. The map allows users to explore survey tracks and view or download selected datasets for further analysis.

Available Datasets

  • BAS Antarctica basemap and coastline: Used as the base layer for all mapping.
  • SPRI/TUD/NSF airborne radar flight lines: Digitized from 1974–75 Ross Ice Shelf surveys.
  • Echogram (Z-scope) data: Browse and download via the interactive map.
  • Power vs. depth (A-scope) data: Browse and download via the interactive map.

About the Project

Frozen Legacies is a collaborative research initiative funded by the National Science Foundation (NSF) to investigate long-term changes beneath Antarctica’s Ross Ice Shelf using both historical and modern radar data.
